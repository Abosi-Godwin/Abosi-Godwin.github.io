My Services

designing

In my design service, I offer a variety of options, including the creation of a Shopify store, email templates, a logo, a social media brand kit, and Hubspot landing page. These are just a few examples of the types of projects I can work on, and I am open to discussing any additional design needs you may have.

Branding

Branding refers to the overall image and identity of a business. This includes elements such as the color scheme, logo, unique selling proposition (USP), and other elements that help a business stand out and be remembered by customers. I can help your business develop a strong brand through the creation of a cohesive color scheme, logo, social media kit, brand policy, and a clear USP and code of conduct (COC). By creating a cohesive and memorable brand, you can turn one-time website visitors into loyal customer.

Marketing

Effective marketing involves reaching and engaging the right audience for your business. This can be achieved through a variety of strategies, including organic and paid advertising, social media marketing, search engine marketing, inbound marketing, conversion rate optimization, and customer relationship management using tools like Hubspot, Manychat, and Sendpulse. To succeed in ecommerce, it's important to attract a large number of interested customers through these methods.

Copywriting

Effective copywriting involves more than just creating SEO-optimized content. It also involves crafting persuasive language that encourages readers to take a desired action. As a professional writer, I offer a range of services including email template copy, product descriptions, landing page copy, social media content, business plans, and ecommerce-related blog posts.
